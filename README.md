Sales Management System – SaleForce Pro

Description:

A professional sales and inventory management system for small businesses. Track products, record sales with customer details, manage stock levels automatically, and view sales history. Includes real-time statistics for total revenue, sales count, and unique customers.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, LocalStorage, Font Awesome

Key Features:

Product inventory management (add, edit, delete)

Record sales with customer name, product, quantity, discount

Automatic stock deduction

Real-time total calculation

Sales history table

Export/Reset functionality
